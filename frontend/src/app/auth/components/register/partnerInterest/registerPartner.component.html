Hello, future partners! Thank you for your interest in becoming a SPEAKHIRE Partner!

This application is for any school or organizations interested in partnering with SPEAKHIRE to bring our programs to your community.

Please answer all questions completely, providing details where needed.

To register, you will need your school and/or organization information, including demographic information and headshot. If you have this information, registration should take about 15 minutes.

If you would like assistance filling out the application, please reach out to us at partners@speakhire.org

As a SPEAKHIRE Partner, you will need to identify faculty and/or staff to serve as SPEAKHIRE Support Liaisons.

We highly recommend that you designate a primary and secondary SPEAKHIRE Support Liaison to best support your community of participants.

Additional administrators can also be involved in our programs as you see fit.
